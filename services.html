Quality

CBS Quality Control Systems enable managers at all levels to evaluate performance on facility management and operational areas with quantifiable metrics and measures. As an ISO 9001:2008 certified company, our Quality Control guidelines are consistent with standards that are internationally tested and recognized. Our procedures evaluate project performance, capture timely deficiency information, inform customers and improve results at all project locations.

Technologies

We control workflow using Computerized Maintenance Management Systems (CMMS), such as Maximo, MicroMain, TRIRIGA, TMA Systems, Facility Center and Archibus. Our Work Control staff is skilled at developing, loading and executing automated Preventive Maintenance, Service Order and Logistical Support Plans, which control, track and record work. Our disciplined Preventive Maintenance Program is integrated into our CMMS and ensures sustained system operations and continuous improvement with proven energy cost reductions.
